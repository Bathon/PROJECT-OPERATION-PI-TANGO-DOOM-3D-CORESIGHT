Floor Plan is a custom Coresight (or PIVision, or PI NewName) symbol which allows users to map an area with a Google Tango enabled device, adding waypoints dynamically. Upon import, PI data points or AF Attributes can be easily associated with the defined waypoints.
Special thanks to Jon Muraski for inspiration and help requisitioning a Tango enabled device.
